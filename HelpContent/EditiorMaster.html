Edition Master
Publication Name
the act or an instance of making information public and any printed work offered for sale or distribution. It may be Magazine or Directory or Newspaper etc.
Edition Name
The entire number of copies of a publication issued at one time or from a single set of type. it may be directory edition or newspaper edition.
Periodicity
Edition issue cycle is mentioned in the field. as some editions have daily periodicity, some have weekly,yearly etc.
UOM
All the created stylesheets are displayed here as ROL stylesheet( matter with logo ads) or DROL stylesheet(normal ads) or CD( 1-2 column image ads).
Publication Center
This is the center from which edition is issued. For example Oil and Gas directory is issued from Dubai so Dubai is the Publication Center of OAGD.
No of Columns, Height, Width, column width
these are the measurements of edition as total number of columns exist on the edition page,Height of the edition and width of the edition.
Gutter Width
this is the gap between the two columns. As OAGD has 0.2 cm gutter width.
