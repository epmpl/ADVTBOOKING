Rate Master
Ad Type
There are two types of Ads. First is Classified ads in which 1 to 2 columns of ad exist. Second is Display Ads in which more than two columns ads exist.Mostly Classified ads are saved in Directory booking.
Ad Category
All ads are categorised specifically as regionwise and rates are defined according to that specific categories.
Packages
All the packages as Oil and gas Directory packages or Yellow Pages packages are displayed in this field. and rates are defined here for the selected dirsctory's packages.
UOM
All the created stylesheets are displayed here as ROL stylesheet( matter with logo ads) or DROL stylesheet(normal ads) or CD( 1-2 column image ads).
Line From & Line To
as in directory, there are some measurements to fetch the rates as according to lines/words/square centimeters etc. these fields assign the range at which rates will be driven.
Color
Ad can be booked black & white or color and mostly both of them have different rates and we can open the rates seperately.
Weekday rate
Base rates will be defined in this field as for one line ad rate/CD rate.
Week Extra Rate
if there are some extra line/size in any ad then that extra line/size rate is mentioned here.
